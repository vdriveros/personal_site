**Live Site:** [Resplandor E-Commerce](https://resplandor.com.py/)

A sophisticated WordPress theme integrated with WooCommerce for creating professional e-commerce stores with modern design and full shopping functionality.

**Description:**
Production-ready WooCommerce theme built for online retailers requiring a feature-rich e-commerce platform. Combines beautiful design with robust e-commerce functionality for selling products online.

**Technologies:** PHP, WordPress, WooCommerce, Tailwind CSS, JavaScript, E-Commerce

**Key Features:**
- Full WooCommerce integration
- Product catalog management
- Shopping cart and checkout optimization
- Payment gateway integration
- Customer account management
- Order tracking system
- Inventory management
- Product reviews and ratings
- Promotional tools (coupons, discounts)
- Responsive mobile-friendly design
- SEO optimized product pages
- Professional storefront design

**E-Commerce Features:**
- Advanced product filtering
- Related products recommendations
- Product image galleries
- Stock management
- Customer testimonials
- Email notifications
- Sales analytics integration
